Getting started

Cora is an all-in-one app for understanding and managing PCOS. You track your cycle, symptoms and habits; follow a cycle-aware movement plan with walk reminders; eat with a PCOS food scanner and recipes; and get gentle insights about what's actually helping — without streaks, shame or ads.
No. Many people use Cora while they're still figuring things out, whether newly diagnosed, years in, or simply suspecting something's off. It's designed to help at any stage.
No. Cora is a wellness and tracking tool. It does not diagnose, treat or prevent any condition, and it is not a substitute for professional medical care. Always consult a qualified clinician about your health.

Tracking & cycles
