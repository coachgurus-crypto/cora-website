This Privacy Policy ("Policy") describes how FunnelStrategist LLC ("Cora," "we," "our," or "us") collects, uses, discloses, and otherwise processes your personal information when you use our Services — including the Cora mobile application, our website, and other interactions you may have with us — as well as your privacy rights and choices.

This Policy forms part of our Terms & Conditions. Terms not defined here have the meaning given in the Terms.

If you reside in the U.S. and are under 13 years of age, you should not use our Services. If we become aware that an individual under 13 may have provided us with personal information, we will investigate and, where appropriate, delete that information and the associated account. If you reside in the European Economic Area, Switzerland, or United Kingdom, please see the Additional Information for Certain Jurisdictions section below.

Personal information we collect

We may collect personal information directly from you, automatically through your use of the Services, and, in limited cases, from third parties.

Information we collect directly from you

Account information. When you create an account, we may collect information such as your first name, email address, and password (stored in hashed form).

Health, cycle & wellness data. Certain features allow you to voluntarily log information about your body and PCOS management. This may include:

  • Menstrual cycle dates, phase information, and related notes;
  • Symptoms, moods, energy levels, and habits you choose to track;
  • Medications, supplements, and treatments;
  • Movement and activity you log, including workout completion and preferences;
  • Food and nutrition information, including meals you scan or enter, dietary notes, and related PCOS scores or insights;
  • Personal notes and other content you choose to save in the app.

This information may be considered sensitive personal information or special category data under applicable law when it relates to your health.

Communications. If you contact us — by email, through our contact form, or otherwise — we collect the information you provide, such as your name, email address, message content, and any attachments.

Payments. If you subscribe to Cora Plus, payment processing is handled by our payment provider (such as Apple). We do not receive or store your full payment card number. We may receive limited billing-related information, such as subscription status, transaction identifiers, and purchase history needed to provide the Service.

Information we collect automatically

When you use the Services, we may collect usage and device data, including:

  • App version, device type, operating system, and language settings;
  • Diagnostic, performance, and crash data to keep the Service reliable and secure;
  • General usage information, such as feature interactions and session activity, often in aggregated or de-identified form;
  • On our website, limited technical data such as IP address, browser type, and pages visited.

We do not require access to your contacts, photo library, or precise location for core features. If an optional feature needs a permission — for example, your camera for the food scanner — we will ask and explain why before you grant it.

Information we receive from third parties

We may receive limited information from service providers that help us operate the Service, such as cloud hosting, analytics, customer support, and payment platforms. We do not purchase lists of personal information from data brokers.

Aggregated, de-identified, or anonymized information

We may aggregate, de-identify, or anonymize information so that it no longer identifies you under applicable law. We may use and disclose such information to understand how the Service is used and to improve it.

How we use personal information

We use personal information to:

  • Provide, personalize, and maintain the Service, including cycle insights, movement plans, nutrition tools, reminders, and doctor-ready summaries;
  • Create and manage your account and sync your data across devices when you are signed in;
  • Process subscriptions and provide customer support;
  • Send service-related communications, such as security alerts, account notices, and responses to your requests;
  • Send optional product updates or tips where permitted by law and, where required, with your consent;
  • Monitor, detect, prevent, and address fraud, abuse, security incidents, and technical issues;
  • Understand usage in aggregate or de-identified form to improve features and reliability;
  • Comply with legal obligations and enforce our Terms.

What we do not do. Cora is funded by subscriptions, not advertising. We do not sell your personal information. We do not share your health data with advertisers, ad networks, or data brokers. We do not run ads inside the app, and we do not use your health information to build advertising profiles about you.

How we disclose personal information

We disclose personal information only as described below:

  • Service providers. Vendors who help us host data, operate infrastructure, analyze reliability, process payments, and provide customer support. They may use your information only to perform services for us under contractual obligations that require appropriate safeguards.
  • At your direction. For example, when you export or share a summary with your clinician or another person you choose.
  • Legal and safety. When we believe disclosure is required by law, regulation, legal process, or governmental request, or to protect the rights, safety, and security of Cora, our users, or others.
  • Business transfers. If we are involved in a merger, acquisition, financing, reorganization, bankruptcy, or sale of assets, personal information may be transferred as part of that transaction, subject to this Policy and any required notice.

We do not disclose your health or cycle data to third parties for their own marketing purposes.

Cookies & similar technologies

Our website may use cookies and similar technologies (such as local storage or pixels) to make the site work, remember preferences, understand general traffic patterns, and improve performance.

Unlike many consumer apps, Cora does not use cookies or in-app tracking for targeted advertising or cross-site profiling. You can manage cookies through your browser settings. If you block certain cookies, some website features may not function properly.

International transfers of personal information

Cora is headquartered in the United States. Our service providers may also process information in the United States and other countries where data protection laws may differ from those in your country of residence.

When we transfer personal information internationally, we use appropriate safeguards as required by applicable law, such as standard contractual clauses. By using the Services, you understand that your information may be processed in countries outside your place of residence.

How we protect your information

We maintain technical, organizational, and administrative safeguards designed to protect personal information against loss, misuse, and unauthorized access, disclosure, alteration, or destruction. These measures include encryption in transit and at rest, access controls, and security reviews.

No method of transmission or storage is completely secure. If you believe your account or data has been compromised, contact us at privacy@cora.app.

How we retain your information

We retain personal information for as long as necessary to provide the Service, fulfill the purposes described in this Policy, comply with legal obligations, resolve disputes, and enforce our agreements.

If you delete your account, we will delete or de-identify your personal and health data within a commercially reasonable period, except where retention is required by law or needed for legitimate business purposes such as fraud prevention, security, or dispute resolution.

Depending on where you live, you may have rights to:
