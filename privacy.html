This Privacy Policy explains how FunnelStrategist LLC ("Cora", "we", "us" or "our") collects, uses, shares and protects information when you use the Cora mobile application and related websites and services (collectively, the "Service"). We've tried to write it in plain language, because your privacy shouldn't require a law degree to understand.

1. Our privacy promise

Cora exists to help you understand your body — not to monetize it. In short:

  • We do not sell your personal or health information.
  • We do not share your data with advertisers or data brokers.
  • We do not run ads inside Cora.
  • You can export or delete your data at any time.

Everything below describes how we live up to that promise.

2. Information we collect

We collect only what we need to provide and improve the Service:

Information you provide

  • Account information: such as your first name, email address and password.
  • Health & cycle data: information you choose to log, including cycle dates, symptoms, moods, energy levels, medications, supplements, treatments and personal notes.
  • Communications: messages you send us, support requests and survey responses.

Information collected automatically

  • Device & usage data: app version, device type, operating system, and limited diagnostic and crash data used to keep the app working.
  • Approximate analytics: aggregated, de-identified usage patterns (for example, which features are used) to improve the Service.

We do not require access to your contacts, photos or precise location to use core features. Where an optional feature needs a permission, we'll ask and explain why.

3. Health & sensitive data

The cycle, symptom and treatment information you log is sensitive personal data, and we treat it with extra care. This information is encrypted in transit and at rest, access is tightly restricted to a limited number of authorized personnel, and it is used only to provide features to you — such as insights, reminders and summaries — and never for advertising.

Cora is a wellness and tracking tool, not a medical provider, and the Service is generally not a "covered entity" under HIPAA. We nonetheless apply strong safeguards to your health information as described in this policy.

4. How we use your information

We use information to:

  • Provide, personalize and maintain the Service, including generating your insights and summaries;
  • Sync your data across your devices when you have an account;
  • Send you service-related communications and, with your consent, optional updates;
  • Diagnose problems, prevent abuse and keep the Service secure;
  • Understand, in aggregate and de-identified form, how the Service is used so we can improve it;
  • Comply with legal obligations.

5. What we never do

We will never sell your personal or health information. We will never share it with advertisers, ad networks or data brokers. We do not use your health data to target advertising, and we do not build advertising profiles about you. If our practices ever change, we will tell you clearly and ask for consent where required.

6. Sharing & disclosure

We share information only in limited circumstances:

  • Service providers: trusted vendors (for example, secure cloud hosting and analytics) who process data on our behalf under contracts that require appropriate protection and prohibit using it for their own purposes.
  • At your direction: for example, when you export a summary to share with your clinician.
  • Legal & safety: when required by law, valid legal process, or to protect the rights, safety and security of our users or the public.
  • Business transfers: if we are involved in a merger, acquisition or sale of assets, your information may be transferred, subject to this policy and any required notice.

7. Data security

We use industry-standard technical and organizational measures to protect your information, including encryption in transit and at rest, access controls and regular security reviews. No method of transmission or storage is ever 100% secure, but we work hard to protect your data and to promptly address any vulnerabilities we discover.

8. Data retention

We retain your information for as long as your account is active or as needed to provide the Service. If you delete your account, we delete or de-identify your personal and health data within a commercially reasonable period, except where we are required to retain certain records to comply with legal obligations.

9. Your rights & choices

Depending on where you live, you may have rights to access, correct, export, restrict or delete your personal information, and to object to certain processing. You can exercise many of these directly in the app, or by contacting us at privacy@cora.app.

For residents of the EEA/UK (GDPR)

Our lawful bases for processing include performance of our contract with you, your consent (which you may withdraw at any time), our legitimate interests in operating and improving the Service, and compliance with legal obligations. You also have the right to lodge a complaint with your local data protection authority.

For California residents (CCPA/CPRA)

We do not sell or "share" personal information as those terms are defined under California law. You have the right to know, delete and correct your personal information and to be free from discrimination for exercising your rights.

10. International data transfers

We may process and store information in countries other than where you live. Where we transfer personal data internationally, we use appropriate safeguards (such as standard contractual clauses) as required by applicable law.

11. Children's privacy

Cora is not directed to children under 13 (or the minimum age required in your jurisdiction), and we do not knowingly collect personal information from them. If you believe a child has provided us information, please contact us and we will delete it.

12. Changes to this policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. If we make material changes, we will notify you through the app or by email before they take effect. The "Last updated" date at the top reflects the most recent revision.
